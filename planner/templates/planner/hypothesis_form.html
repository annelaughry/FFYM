Form Your Hypothesis

Based on your research question, identify your variables and state your hypothesis.

What is a Hypothesis?

A hypothesis is a clear statement that can be confimed through a test or experiment.
It should be well reasoned and rooted in factual information from backround research.

A Hypothesis is not:
  • A guess
  • An opinion
  • A prediction

How to Form Your Hypothesis

The most straightforward method is to idenfify your independent and dependent variables and create an "if/then statement." As you become more comfortable forming hypotheses, your statements can take differnt form, but should still include the expected effect of the independent variable on the dependent.

Example:
  • Research Question: How does the amount of nitrogen in soil effect how fast a plant grows?
  • Independent variable: The amout of nitogen in the soil.
  • Dependent Variable: Plant growth rate.
  • Hypothesis: If a soil sample has a higher amount of nitrogen, then plants will grow faster.
